Aurora University


Computer Science Association

Mission

We are an organization that brings students with technological interests together to share and explore different perspectives of Computer Science.

Purpose

We strive to prepare Computer Science students for life after graduation by connecting with different networks as well as providing a supporting environment while having an enjoyable time.

The AU CSC club officers meet on Wednesdays at noon in the Birck center. These meeting are open to any member who would like to help organize club events.

The club is open to Any student enrolled in at least three (3) credits with Aurora University.

Aurora University Computer Science Association is committed to the elimination of discrimination based on gender, race, class, economic status, ethnic background, sexual orientation, age, physical ability, and cultural and religious backgrounds
